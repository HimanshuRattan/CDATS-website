Our Vision


We at CDATS (Centre for Data Driven Automation Technologies and Systems) aim at bringing revolutionary changes
in the current systems, by applying robust and automated technical solutions to real world problems. Having successfully
delivered many fully functional and industry ready projects in the last one year in different domains like
online management systems, automation systems, high performance simulation etc. We would like to expand our
boundaries and provide students a platform to gain real world project exposure and work on cutting edge projects.
